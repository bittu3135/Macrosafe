ABOUT MACROSAFE
Macrosafe Technology Co.,Ltd is a high-tech enterprise, which specializing in manufacturing and marketing of pedestrian turnstile gates and turnstile access control system since 2010. Macrosafe products have been exported to over 80 countries worldwide,including
corporate areas, entertainment venues,government buildings,public transportation and so on.
With its stable and high-quality products as well as timely and excellent services, Macrosafe has gained good reputation and sincere trust from our customers at home and abroad.
With an experienced R&D team and professional sales representatives, Macrosafe always offer 24-7 support (through video, email, picture and online communication) for customers. All Macrosafe products will be provided at least 2 years warranty and lifetime maintenance.
