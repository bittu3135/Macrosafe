Construction Site Turnstiles
The construction site access control system, just as its name implies, is to set up a access control system at the entrance and exit of construction site. It combines  construction turnstiles which can opened by swiping card, password, fingerprint, face recognition and other verification methods, and video capture technology to strengthen the management of the entry and exit, and attendance of construction site workers. At the same time, it can count the number of people entering and exiting and display personnel information in real time. Thus construction site access help to prevent non-employees from entering, ensures higher working quality and the safety of property.

RFID/Fingerprint/Coin Turnstile System
The intelligent access control system based on RFID or fingerprint recognition mainly provides access control of the entrances and exits where high and unmanned monitoring security is required. The working process is: when the user swipes the card to open the turnstile gate (door), the card reader reads the information in the card, the main microcontroller compares the information with the internal database information, and then judges the cardholder's authority and controls the turnstile (door) opens. At the same time, the host computer displays and stores card information and operating information. The user can also enter the password through the keyboard to open the turnstile gate (door).

ESD turnstile gate system
In many places where electrostatic protection areas (EPA) are set up, the testing of electrostatic footwear and electrostatic wristbands is carried out by traditional manual supervision, which is not easy to implement, and even makes mistakes. It is difficult to gain the trust of customers and management. There is also a great potential danger of static electricity from personnel to the ground. To solve these problems, macrosafe provides an ESD static detection access control system that ensures operators are actually prepared for personnel electrostatic grounding when entering the EPA area; and combined with the turnstile gate as a pedestrian passage control equipment. It can provide an orderly and civilized way for people entering and exiting, prevent illegal entry and exit, and quickly open the gate in emergency situations to organize evacuation. When designing this scheme, we fully consider the protection of the product quality through this system to the greatest extent possible.
