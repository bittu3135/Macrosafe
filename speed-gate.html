Speed Gate Definition:

Speed gate also called fastlane gate, is a technologically advanced security turnstile that combines aesthetics and security. With its sleek and silm design, speed gate is one of the essential components of modern access control.
Speed Gate Variations:
A lot of variations of designs are available with speed gate due to their technological advancement. In addition to standard height barriers, ultra-high anti-climbing barriers are available to further enhance security. And its built-in infrared sensors are capable of accurately detecting user behavior. There will be an alarm triggered if entry is not granted.
Speed Gate Applications:
The sleek design and high throughput of speed gates make them popular for use in fitness centers, stadiums, commercial lobbys, offices, cultural centers, hotels, and more.
