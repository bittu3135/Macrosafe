Swing Barrier Definition:

Swing Barrier (also call swing turnstile, optical turnstile, speed gate), is a form of access control turnstiles that automates the management of people flow at entrance and leave checkpoints, preventing unwanted visits and enhancing building security. The spacious passageways are accessible not only to ordinary people, but also for wheelchair users, people with crutches, strollers, bicycles or tool transport, etc.
Swing Barrier Variations:

Passage width can be made from 550mm to 1200mm according to project needs.
Pattern, finish, material, gate size, barrier height, etc. can be customized.

Swing Barrier Applications:
With a modern and user-friendly design, swing barrier is a perfect option for a range of facilities seeking barrier-free access control and security. Such as office buildings, shopping malls, restaurants, entertainment facilities, hotels, fitness clubs, airports, subways, museums, cinemas, customs, etc.
